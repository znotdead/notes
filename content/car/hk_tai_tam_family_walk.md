### HK: Tai Tam Family Walk
**Route:**
**Length:** 1.6km (by GPS 2 km)
**Level:** Easy
**Getting to:**
 - bus to Parkview

**Getting from:**
 - bus from Parkview

**Attractions:**
 - Views HK
 - reservoir
 - forest and stream

**Group:** Me, Alex and Erica(3y 5m)
**Our Time:** 1h
**Date:** 11 September 2016

**Condition:**
Not Accessible with stroller. 40m elevation gain. Mostly shaded.

**Notes:**
No Toilets. Picnic area at start.
